This course will cover topics that will be seen on the ACT as well as help provide students with test-taking strategies that can be applied to each ACT problem.

Math

Students will refine their understanding of the fundamental properties of algebra including exponent rules, equation writing and solving, inequalities, systems of equations, quadratics, and functions. Geometry topics including triangles, circles, angles, and trigonometry will also be discussed as well as topics on statistics and interpreting data. Students will learn what keywords to look for in a word problem as well as other methods for efficient time management.

Reading & Writing

Students will refine their understanding of sentence structure, vocabulary, critical reading, writing error revision, etc. Students will learn common grammar patterns to look for as well as other methods for efficient time management.

Learning Objectives

Refine test-taking skills through frequent practice tests and review.

Review algebraic, geometric, and statistical topics that will be covered on the SAT in order to develop strong fundamental math skills needed for this test.

Refine understanding of sentence structure, English grammar, and how to identify common writing errors that will be covered on the SAT in order to develop strong fundamental English skills needed for this test.

Identify areas in which students are weak and discuss several strategies to deal with these weak areas.
